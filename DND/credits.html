Credits

In addition to the credits for Ghosts of Saltmarsh, here are the people responsible for the other pre-made adventures I've been using (with links where I have them):

From the Lonely Scroll Adventure Contest: Saltmarsh

  • The adventure with Slate was A Heart of Stone, by Kerry Jordan.
  • The Church of St. Eadrine was The Shapeshifers' Prison, by David S. Percival, map by Dyson Logos.
  • The Quipper Bait/Fehr job was from Essence of Tharizdun, by Johan Hedin.

From Encounters on the Savage Seas

  • The Templars of the Trident were from Upon the Crashing Waves, by Ashley Warren, map by Dyson Logos.
  • The goblin raft ambush was GFS Bad-Stingy, by Alex Clippinger, art by Daniel Walthall.
  • The flying fish were Life Aquatic..., specifically Flying Blind, by Jean Lorber.
  • The Sahuagin zoo was Trouble in the Tide Pools, by Hannah Rose, map by Jeff C. Stevens.

From Encounters in the Savage Frontier

  • The hill giant fight was To'bar by Jeff C. Stevens. The map I used was by Dice Grimorium.

Other art and maps:

  • The map for the Bullywug ambush was by Neutral Party.
  • The Shambling Mound ambush map was by Dice Grimorium.
  • The map used for the Thousand-Teeth fight was by GoAdventureMaps.
  • The new maps for Serenity as well as the map I used for the Lizardfolk Lair were by Eric Blancho.
