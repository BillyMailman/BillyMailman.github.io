Credits

In addition to the credits for Ghosts of Saltmarsh, here are the people responsible for the other pre-made adventures I've been using (with links where I have them):

From the Lonely Scroll Adventure Contest: Saltmarsh

  • The adventure with Slate was A Heart of Stone, by Kerry Jordan.
  • The Church of St. Eadrine was The Shapeshifers' Prison, by David S. Percival, map by Dyson Logos.
  • The Quipper Bait/Fehr job was from Essence of Tharizdun, by Johan Hedin.
  • The attack on Serenity and Gellan's ships was Fire on the Water, Darion Smith. The map was an unrelated map of Saltmarsh Harbour by Eric Blancho.
  • The kidnapping by Golbuldoolpulg was Golbuldoolpulg, by Jay Africa
  • The rescue of Ferrin from the blue dragon was Like a Fish Out of Water by Chris Cascioli, map by Dyson Logos.

From Encounters on the Savage Seas

  • The Templars of the Trident were from Upon the Crashing Waves, by Ashley Warren, map by Dyson Logos.
  • The goblin raft ambush was GFS Bad-Stingy, by Alex Clippinger, art by Daniel Walthall.
  • The flying fish were Life Aquatic..., specifically Flying Blind, by Jean Lorber.
  • The trading dolphins were also from Life Aquatic..., specifically Dolphin Trick-or-Treat, by Jean Lorber.
  • The Sahuagin zoo was Trouble in the Tide Pools, by Hannah Rose, map by Jeff C. Stevens.
  • The kraken cult were Song of the One Below, by Michael E. Shea, map by Daniel Walthall.
  • Greta Amberhair was Greta Amberhair and the Water Wagon, by Jeff C. Stevens, map by Dante Ezio Cifaldi.

From Encounters on the Savage Seas II

  • The Nostalgia for the Reef was from Nostalgia for the Reef by Elven Tower, map by same.

From Encounters in the Savage Frontier

  • The hill giant fight was To'bar by Jeff C. Stevens. The map I used was by Dice Grimorium.
  • The Stregone family tomb was Tomb of Lost Hope and Joy, by Ken Carcas, map by Daniel F. Walthall.

From Encounters in the Savage Wilderness

  • The rescue of the Tizzles was The Great Devourer, by Christopher Walz, with map by same.

Other art and maps:

  • The map for the Bullywug ambush was by Neutral Party.
  • The Shambling Mound ambush map was by Dice Grimorium.
  • The map used for the Thousand-Teeth fight was by GoAdventureMaps.
  • The newer VTT maps for Serenity as well as the map I used for the Lizardfolk Lair, the one I used for the Emperor of the Waves, and any other GoS content we run in Roll20/Foundry, honestly, are/were all by Eric Blancho.
  • The cannons on Serenity are modified from cannon tokens by 2 Minute Tabletop.
