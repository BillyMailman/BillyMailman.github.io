These are some pretty useful reagent combos, that make for great multi-effect potions

Bugloss, Columbine, Mountain Flower
Creates a potion with Restore Health, Restore Stamina, and Restore Magicka. Restore Health is on all three items, and is thus boosted.
Corn Flower, Lady's Smock, Namira's Rot
Creates a potion with Restore Magicka, Increase Spell Power, and Increase Spell Critical. Great for sustaining Magicka-heavy builds.
Blessed Thistle, Dragonthorn, Water Hyacinth
Creates a potion with Restore Stamina, Increase Weapon Power, and Increase Weapon Critical. Serves as the stamina counterpart of the previous potion, and is similarly great for Stamina-heavy builds.
Columbine, Dragonthorn, Wormwood
Creates a potion with Unstoppable, Weapon Critical, and Restore Stamina. Not a lot of synergy, but still three good effects.
